P.M.C

(Project Management Consultancy)

As builders and contractors, we used to face several problems while executing construction projects. We realized that very few construction organizations had systematic approach to project execution. They often overlooked basic site set-up like labour hutments, water provision and storage, power arrangement, machinery and equipment, manpower planning, etc. This disturbs systematic and organized work on the site leading to wastage of time, effort, energy and money. This experience led Needs to specialize in Project Management Consultancy. We provide a wide range of Project Management Consultancy services from managing the construction of a project to directing the entire process from conception to completion. PMC helps relieve client of most exceptional responsibilities of the project. It enables the client to focus on his core business and tasks.
