FACULTY OF M.P.I.L

Mothers Pride Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. Was Established In 2014 with the objective of establishing a sustainable and continually improving consultancy company.

Mr. Rakesh Kumar Singh, M.Tech (Structure) BHUIT, Varanasi founded the company with a dream to provide all construction solution under one roof. Honest effort and efficient delivery translated this dream into reality within a short span of time and now the company is offering multi-disciplinary services in the area of :-

  • Engineering Design
  • Project Planning & Management
  • Quality Assurance
  • Project supervision
  • Measurement Audit
  • Geotechnical Investigation
  • Preliminary & Detail Survey
